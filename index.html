Who We Are

Rooted in Nature,
Growing with You

Varshaana Nursery is a trusted wholesale plant nursery in North Boothinatham, Udumalaipettai, Tamil Nadu. We supply ornamentals, flowering plants, fruit trees, grass turf and more to landscapers, gardeners and bulk buyers across the region.

We specialise in plants, landscaping garden work and garden maintenance — bringing greenery and beauty to homes, offices and commercial spaces across Tamil Nadu.
